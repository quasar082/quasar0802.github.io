Motion becomes valuable when it helps the user understand sequence, emphasis, and continuity.

In product design, the best animation usually feels almost invisible. It guides attention, confirms an action, or reveals a relationship between two states.

This is especially important in AI interfaces where content can change quickly and asynchronously. Without motion cues, updates can feel abrupt or confusing.
